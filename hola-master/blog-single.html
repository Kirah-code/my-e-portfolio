Narrative Report

Event Title: Peer Wellness Awareness Session

Date: July 18, 2025

Location: TUP-Taguig Guidance Office AVR

Presenters: Ariane Reyes, Joshua De Leon, Bianca Torres

Observers: Ms. Elena Villanueva (Guidance Counselor), Mr. Leo Santos (PFG Adviser)

Introduction

The Peer Wellness Awareness Session was held to strengthen the capacity of student leaders in supporting the mental health needs of their peers. The event focused on identifying stress indicators, active listening, and peer response techniques.

Highlights of the Event

  • Opening remarks by Ms. Elena Villanueva
  • Interactive presentation on "Peer Listening Techniques"
  • Live role-playing of peer support scenarios
  • Small group reflections facilitated by presenters

Observers’ Feedback

Ms. Villanueva praised the presenters' clarity and composure throughout the session. Mr. Santos noted that the activity demonstrated the students’ readiness to serve as peer facilitators and contribute to the university’s mental health initiatives.

Conclusion

The activity concluded with a commitment to continue peer-led mental health awareness efforts. Feedback was gathered and certificates of participation were distributed to both attendees and speakers.
