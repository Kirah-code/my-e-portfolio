Narrative Report

Event Title: Peer Wellness Awareness Session

Date: July 18, 2025

Location: TUP-Taguig Guidance Office AVR

Presenters: Ariane Reyes, Joshua De Leon, Bianca Torres

Observers: Ms. Elena Villanueva (Guidance Counselor), Mr. Leo Santos (PFG Adviser)

Introduction

In the final week of our scheduled demo teaching series, Week 10 was allocated for late or revised submissions from classmates. I acted as an observer and peer feedback contributor. This week served as a culmination of our learning journey, where all participants had the opportunity to refine and finalize their teaching performances. As I watched the submitted demos, I noticed a significant improvement in pacing and visual presentation compared to earlier weeks. My comments to peers included positive reinforcement on their improved interaction strategies, particularly in using callouts and question-and-answer segments. However, I also noted areas for development, such as the need for smoother transitions and clearer voice modulation. Additionally, I recommended the inclusion of time markers to guide lesson flow and suggested integrating student-led activities to promote learner-centered teaching. Through this experience, I further strengthened my ability to evaluate instructional strategies, offer constructive feedback, and reflect critically on my own teaching performance.

Observers’ Feedback

Ms. Villanueva praised the presenters' clarity and composure throughout the session. Mr. Santos noted that the activity demonstrated the students’ readiness to serve as peer facilitators and contribute to the university’s mental health initiatives.
