Narrative Report

Event Title: Peer Wellness Awareness Session

Date: July 18, 2025

Location: TUP-Taguig Guidance Office AVR

Presenters: Ariane Reyes, Joshua De Leon, Bianca Torres

Observers: Ms. Elena Villanueva (Guidance Counselor), Mr. Leo Santos (PFG Adviser)

Introduction

In Week 8, I submitted a late individual teaching demo due to a previous scheduling conflict. To comply with the course requirement, I created a recorded teaching session covering one of the basic skills in Home Economics. The demo was 10 to 12 minutes long and shared through a video link for evaluation. I aimed to apply all prior feedback by ensuring clear objectives, structured content, and the use of supporting visuals. However, observer feedback indicated that while the content was well-structured and informative, my delivery needed improvement. I was advised to work on voice modulation, maintain consistent eye contact with the camera, and avoid reading directly from the slides. There was also a note that some of my slides were text-heavy and could benefit from more images or icons. I took this as an opportunity to improve my script by converting full paragraphs into bullet points and began practicing voice projection and pacing using short rehearsal recordings. I also adjusted my camera position to better simulate face-to-face interaction, which helped me appear more confident and present during virtual teaching.

Observers’ Feedback

Ms. Villanueva praised the presenters' clarity and composure throughout the session. Mr. Santos noted that the activity demonstrated the students’ readiness to serve as peer facilitators and contribute to the university’s mental health initiatives.
